    About us and our mission

What is accessible tourism?

There are varied and evolving definitions of the term ‘Accessible Tourism’.

To us, it’s simply the quest to ensure tourism destinations and products are available to everyone. That means, regardless of age or disability, visitors should be able to share the same – or similar – experience to everyone else.

We’re not academics – we’re communicators, and what we know – is that there is a distinct lack of information available for disabled traveller. That’s what we want to fix.

If you want to dive into the definitions and details; The European Network for Accessible Tourism (ENAT) has a page attempting to provide some clarity on the matter.
